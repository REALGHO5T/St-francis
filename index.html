What is expression?

Expressions is St. Francis' College, Lucknow's premier inter-school cultural extravaganza that brings together talented students from across the city. This annual celebration of creativity and artistic excellence provides a dynamic platform for young minds to showcase their talents across various disciplines including dance, music, theatre, fine arts, and literary events.
With a rich legacy spanning several years, Expressions has become synonymous with innovation, competition, and cultural exchange among school communities. The event features meticulously curated competitions, workshops, and performances that challenge participants to push their creative boundaries while fostering meaningful connections with peers from different institutions. Through this grand celebration, St. Francis' College aims to nurture artistic expression, build confidence, and create lasting memories for all participants. Join us as we continue this tradition of excellence and artistic exploration in 2024.

About the School

St Francis School, established in 1885 in Lucknow, stands as a beacon of educational excellence. With over 135 years of rich heritage, the institution has shaped countless young minds through holistic development, academic rigor, and strong moral values. Our legacy of nurturing future leaders continues to inspire generations.
